🔥 HIGH DAYS (Mon / Wed / Fri)

Massive CNS output. Sprints + Gym on the same day. Do not skip.

Monday: Max Velocity & Leg Mass

  • 🏃‍♂️

    Track: T-Apex Assisted Overspeed (45m)

    Warm-Up: 5-6 min aerobic jog + dynamics + 3x30m Potentiation build-ups.
    Workout: T-Apex assisted runs (running toward unit). Teach the brain to remove velocity inhibition.

  • 🏋️‍♂️

    Gym: Leg Hypertrophy (60m)

    Back Squats (4x8), Romanian Deadlifts (4x8), Bulgarian Split Squats (3x10/leg), Leg Curls (3x12), Calf Raises (4x15).

Wednesday: Acceleration & Power

  • 💨

    Field & Garage: T-Apex Explosive Mechanics (45m)

    Field: T-Apex Resisted Sprints (running away).
    Garage: T-Apex Single-Leg Jump Squats (3x5/leg) + T-Apex Explosive Alternating Chest Press (3x8/arm) + T-Apex Resisted Knee Drives (3x8/leg).

  • Gym: Trunk & Arm Hypertrophy (60m)

    Weighted Pull-ups (4x8), Seated DB Shoulder Press (4x8), Incline DB Press (3x10), DB Rows (3x10), Bicep/Tricep supersets (3x12).

Friday: Translation & Raw Strength

  • 🏁

    Track: Unassisted Free Sprints (45m)

    No T-Apex. Translate your overspeed and acceleration gains into natural free sprinting mechanics.

  • 🏗️

    Gym: Force Production (45m)

    Trap Bar Deadlift (3x4), Front Squats (3x5), Weighted Dips (3x6), Weighted Chin-ups (3x6). Low reps, pure strength.

🌿 LOW DAYS (Tue / Thu / Sat / Sun)

Complete Rest. Your muscles grow while you rest. The CNS needs 48 hours to clear the fatigue from your High Days. Light walking, stretching, or massage only. Zero heavy lifting.
